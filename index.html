Welcome to My Cinnamon Roll Fan Site!

This is a site dedicated to my favorite baked good, the cinnamon roll.

My Favorite Things About Cinnamon Rolls:

  1. They are filled with the most delicious spice, cinnamon
  2. They are warm and delicious - perfect for a cozy morning meal
  3. The best cinnamon rolls have cream cheese frosting, which is my favorite frosting
  4. They have many delicious layers
